Tulang Belakang Sdn Bhd, Malaysia, is a B2C company that markets its high-quality socks directly to consumers on the e-commerce platform. We target customers looking for comfortable yet beautiful socks that serve both fashion and functional purposes.

In addition, by taking an Internet-based approach, shopping becomes easy and very convenient for our customers around the globe. Our e-commerce site enables browsing through our products and ordering from anywhere with home delivery. This also makes the link direct enough for us to retain a personal relationship with our buyers, consumer-level quality, and satisfaction.

This direct engagement helps us maintain a personal connection with our buyers while ensuring quality control and customer satisfaction. Our B2C strategy permits great visibility into developing consumer preferences across the fashion-apparel industry, allowing swift adaptation to trends.
